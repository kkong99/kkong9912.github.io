Welcome!

This is a website designed by teens for teens. It showcases many different low cost activities and places for teens to go during their free time. The activities range from artistic things to outdoor activities. Feel free to explore all the boroughs in New York to find a perfect hangout for you and all your friends! You can even add an event that you know about and want to share with other teens around the city.
