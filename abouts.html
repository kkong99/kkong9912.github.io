About Us!

Who are we?

Hi, we are Kimora, Chynna, Jasmine, and Lissette. We are high school students in New York City. We all participated in the 2015 AOL CUNY Advanced Science Research Center Girls Who Code summer immersion program. Girls Who Code is an intensive seven week program where rising juniors and seniors can learn different coding languages. As our final project, we decided to created this website, Teens Today, using the skills we learned in HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Why Teens Today?

We decided to create Teens Today because teenagers are always on their phones. We made this website to bring back human interaction. Technology has managed to take over our lives, our mission is to get teens back outside, away from their phones, and talking to each other.
